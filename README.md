## ‚ú® Features

* **Native Performance:** Built with Swift & SwiftUI. Zero lag.
* **Mouse Avoidance:** The light smartly "dodges" your cursor so you can click buttons in the corners.
* **Customizable:** Adjust width (up to 120px) and color.
* **Presets:** Includes Warm, Cool, Neon, Gold, and Matrix themes.
* **Menu Bar Friendly:** Respects your screen space, doesn't overlap the top menu or Dock.

## üñ• System Requirements

* **macOS 13.5** or later (Ventura, Sonoma, Sequoia, Tahoe).
* Works on both Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) and Intel Macs.

## üì• Download & Install

1. Go to the [**Releases Page**](https://github.com/nrksu1tan/TahoeGlow/releases) and download `TahoeGlow.zip`.
2. Unzip and move the app to your **Applications** folder.
3. **Right-click** (or Control-click) on the app icon and select **Open** (required for the first launch).
